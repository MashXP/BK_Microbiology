    * **Rhizarians (Radiolarians, Forams, Cercozoans):** Amoebas with threadlike pseudopodia. Example: *Globigerina*.

### Page 94

* **Concept 28.4 (Archaeplastida):** Red and green algae are closest relatives of plants.
    * **Red Algae:** Phycoerythrin pigment. Examples: *Porphyridium*, *Palmaria*.
    * **Green Algae:** Plant-type chloroplasts. Examples: *Chlamydomonas*, *Ulva*.
    * **Plants:** Mosses, ferns, conifers, flowering plants.
* **Concept 28.5 (Unikonts):** Closely related to fungi and animals.
    * **Amoebozoans (Tubulinids, Slime molds, Entamoebas):** Amoebas with lobe-shaped or tube-shaped pseudopodia. Examples: *Amoeba*, *Dictyostelium*.
    * **Opisthokonts:** Nucleariids, Choanoflagellates, Animals, Fungi.
